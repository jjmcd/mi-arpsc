5.1. Introduction

In order to obtain meaningful data, we need to all stick to the same standards.

5.2. Frequency

All transmissions will occur on 3583.75 kHz center frequency, or as close as practical. This means you will set your transceiver to 3583 or 3582, USB, and place the waterfall cursor on 3583.75 . Setting your transceiver to 3582 would put this frequency close to the center of your passband, but some software forces the location of the MT63 cursor, requiring the 3583 setting. Be sure to familiarize yourself with your equipment ahead of time.

5.3. Power Output

All Stations will transmit with an output power of 20 watts PEP. Antenna differences will obviously cause differences in ERP.

5.4. Modes

Four modes will be used during the test:
  • BPSK-31
  • Olivia - 16/500
  • MT-63 - 500 long
  • MFSK - 16
