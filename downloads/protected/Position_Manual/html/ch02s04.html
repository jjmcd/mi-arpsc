2.4. Debrief

At the end of the exercise, there will be a debrief from all agencies. Prior to this check with the station operators to be sure you are aware of any contact with the site or nearby jurisdictions.
Make a positive, brief presentation when asked. As usual, avoid technical details and focus on capability.
