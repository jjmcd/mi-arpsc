3. E-Team

3.1. Introduction

As a general rule, the station staff maintains the RACES Situation Report (SitRep) since the SEC frequently does not have a terminal. However, if for some reason the station staff is unable to maintain the SitRep, it falls on the SEC or his delegate to do so.
It is important to many agencies to be alert to the state of readiness of the RACES station. Agencies have discovered that their communications circuits are sometimes fragile, and they may require our services. An up to date and accurate SitRep gives them some confidence about our status.
In any case, be sure the Agency SitRep is complete, accurate, and up to date.

3.2. Logging On

  • Log on to E-Team using the password provided by the DIT rep (typically Rich Law or Jacqueline Barcroft). They will usually log you on.

    Use SEOC-specific password

     
    Do not use your personal password when logging on at the SEOC.

3.3. Initial Duties

  • Review the incident report
  • Add an Agency SitRep

3.4. Repetitive Duties

  • Update the agency sitrep approximately hourly (generally the station operators manage this).
  • Add Duty Log entries if needed
  • Review the incident report and other agency SitReps
